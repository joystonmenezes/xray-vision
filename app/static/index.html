How it works

1 · Preprocess

The image is resized to 256×256 with its aspect ratio preserved, center-cropped to 224×224, and normalized — the same pipeline used at training time.

2 · Classify

A DenseNet-121, trained on ~37k musculoskeletal radiographs from the Stanford MURA dataset, outputs the probability that the study is abnormal.

3 · Explain

SmoothGrad averages input gradients across dozens of noise-perturbed copies of the image to localize the region of abnormality.

⚠️ Research & demonstration only — not a medical device and not for clinical use.

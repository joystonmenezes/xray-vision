How it works

Three steps, all running locally on your device.

1

Preprocess

Your image is resized and normalized the same way the model saw its training data, so it sees a familiar input.

2

Classify

A DenseNet-121 averages several views of the scan (flip, small rotations, zoom) to estimate the chance of an abnormality.

3

Explain

An occlusion sensitivity map highlights the region driving the prediction, so the result is never a black box.

Try it yourself
